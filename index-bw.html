Who We Are

About Us

Dark Holler Comics® is a digital comics publishing company. The publishing platform used by DHC is called the MiMEngine (Motion Interactive Messaging Experience). Normal comic book conventions, such as traditional comic books or graphic novels don't translate well on common devices like modern phones. In order to target the most commonly used devices the comic book format has to change. Dark Holler Comics® champions a solution for publishing sequential art media on common devices for an advanced interactive experience. More than just a PDF reader, the publishing platform supports creators that want to incorporate audio, animation, and interactions.

The MiMEngine, an open source publishing platform built on Google Cloud uses the Flutter SDK to produce apps for iOS and Android that storytellers can use to craft stories, train AI characters within their stories, and publish MiMEs (books with AI characters) to gain readership and build their business.
